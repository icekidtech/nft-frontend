About AstralPackLegends

The Collection

AstralPackLegends is a unique collection of 104 cosmic-themed NFTs living on the Lisk Sepolia testnet. Each NFT represents a celestial entity from our vast universe, from stars and planets to cosmic anomalies and ethereal beings.

The collection was created by a team of artists passionate about space, astronomy, and blockchain technology. Our goal is to bring the wonder of the cosmos to the digital realm through these collectible artworks.

Minting Process

When you mint an AstralPackLegends NFT, you'll receive a randomly selected token from the unminted pool. Each NFT has its own unique traits, rarity, and cosmic story to tell.

The minting process is simple and user-friendly. Connect your wallet, navigate to the Mint page, and click the Mint button. Your NFT will be revealed instantly after the transaction is confirmed.

Technology

AstralPackLegends is built on the Lisk Sepolia testnet, a secure and efficient blockchain designed for digital collectibles. All metadata is stored on IPFS, ensuring your cosmic collectibles will remain accessible for generations to come.

Our smart contract implements ERC-721 standards and has been thoroughly audited for security. The random minting algorithm ensures a fair distribution of all NFTs in the collection.
